Overview of Salesforce CRM

Salesforce is a leading customer relationship management (CRM) platform that provides a comprehensive suite of tools for managing customer interactions and data. It includes several key components:

  • Salesforce CRM: A cloud-based platform that helps businesses manage customer relationships, sales processes, and marketing efforts.
  • Salesforce Lightning: A modern user interface that provides enhanced customization and usability features for building and deploying applications on the Salesforce platform.
  • Salesforce Apex: A proprietary programming language used for custom development on the Salesforce platform, enabling advanced business logic and integrations.
  • Salesforce Visualforce: A framework for building custom user interfaces and experiences within the Salesforce ecosystem, integrating seamlessly with Salesforce data and processes.
